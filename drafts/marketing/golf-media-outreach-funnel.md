# Golf media outreach funnel

Goal: find golf audiences that already reach pool runners, fantasy golf players, club golfers, buddies-trip groups, and weekend golf sickos. First pass is pricing discovery. Do not over-polish the pitch yet.

## What we are selling

Golf Pools Pro is a cleaner app for major-week golf pools. Hosts set up a pool, players join free, make picks, and follow a live leaderboard all week.

Best short points:

- Built for golf pools, not stale office-pool software.
- Players join free.
- First 5 entries are free.
- First pool is capped at $9 for the launch offer.
- Works for U.S. Open groups, clubs, bars, buddies trips, and office pools.
- Live leaderboard all week.

Do not use betting, payout, buy-in, wagering, or purse language.

## Target buckets

1. Fantasy golf podcasts and newsletters
2. PGA Tour preview podcasts
3. Weekend-golfer and golf culture podcasts
4. Golf blogs with U.S. Open / fantasy / pool content
5. Golf meme and social pages
6. Local or regional golf newsletters
7. Golf club, simulator, and indoor golf communities
8. Buddies-trip sites and newsletters
9. Bar/restaurant golf promo accounts near major weeks
10. Golf Facebook groups where promos are allowed

## Pricing hypothesis before quotes

Use this only as a working guess until we get real quotes.

- Small niche golf podcast host-read: $100-$500
- Mid-size golf podcast host-read: $500-$2,000
- Newsletter sponsored blurb: $150-$1,000
- Strong niche newsletter send: $1,000-$3,000
- Blog sponsored post: $250-$1,500
- Social page story/post package: $50-$750
- Larger golf media package: $3,000-$10,000+
- Affiliate or rev-share: worth testing only if attribution is clean

Best first bet: small and mid-size creators with golf-pool/fantasy audiences. We want people who can say, "I would actually use this for my U.S. Open pool."

## What to ask for

For each outlet, ask for:

- Media kit
- Sponsorship options
- U.S. Open week availability
- Price for one placement
- Price for a package
- Audience size
- Audience location
- Podcast downloads / newsletter open rate / average post views
- Whether they can do a quick product demo or host-read mention
- Whether their audience runs golf pools, fantasy leagues, club events, or buddies trips
- Deadlines for copy/assets

## Best-fit placements

Top priority:

- Host-read podcast mention before U.S. Open
- Newsletter blurb with direct CTA
- Social story/post with screen recording or simple image ad
- Blog mention in U.S. Open pool/fantasy content

Lower priority:

- Generic display ads
- Broad golf equipment audiences
- Expensive national media with weak attribution
- Any placement that forces betting language

## Booking rules

Book only if at least one is true:

- Audience clearly includes fantasy golf / pool runners / club golfers.
- Creator is willing to explain the product in their own voice.
- Placement happens before or during U.S. Open week.
- Price is low enough to treat as a data test.

Pass if:

- They only sell broad display inventory.
- They require gambling/payout language.
- They cannot give audience numbers.
- They want a large package before we have response data.
