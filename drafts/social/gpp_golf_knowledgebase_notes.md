# GPP golf knowledgebase notes

Purpose: keep the GPP social system grounded in real current golf conversation instead of generic golf jokes.

## What to collect
- Specific golfers being discussed: name, reason, source URL, whether it is current news or evergreen.
- Rules/penalty controversies: what happened, why golfers are arguing, safe angle.
- Course setup/layout debates: course feature, complaint/praise, why it matters.
- Training/swing-method debates: method name, promise, skepticism, safe joke/angle.
- Equipment/gear debates: product/category, claim, skeptic angle.
- Pace/etiquette disputes: situation, public-golf social norm, safe observation.

## What not to collect
- Generic vibe buckets like “general golf chatter.”
- Reddit wording to reuse verbatim.
- Low-context image posts with no usable topic.
- Betting/parlay/crypto/spam.

## Output standard
A good sampler report should show actual subjects, not just categories:
- `Garrick Higgo parts ways with caddie after PGA Championship penalty`
- `Adam Scott's 100th consecutive major`
- `course vandalism / bunker-green damage debate`
- `club fitting as an 18 handicap`
- `penalty vs playing out of hazard`

A bad report says only:
- `general golf chatter`
- `tour/player arguments`
- generic draft joke with no source subject
